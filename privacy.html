Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-17 23:18 UTC

This Privacy Policy explains how the Workday Timer widget (“Service”) handles data when you connect your Notion workspace.

What we access

  • Notion databases you authorize, typically Clients, Projects, and Time Log.
  • Basic metadata necessary to create/read time entries you request.

What we store

  • Access token: stored locally in localStorage in your browser. It is sent with each request you initiate.
  • No server-side storage: we do not persist your token or content on our server. Netlify processes requests transiently.

What we write to Notion

When you click Pause/Stop, we create a row in your Time Log with date, minutes, and relations to the chosen Project and Client. Optional write-back can update a numeric total on the Project.

Analytics & cookies

No analytics are built in. The widget uses localStorage for preferences (theme, weekly goal, most recent selection). No third‑party cookies are set by the widget.

Subprocessors

  • Netlify – hosts this site and runs serverless functions.
  • Notion – API provider you authorize.

Data retention

We don’t keep copies of your workspace data. Your time entries live inside your Notion databases until you delete them.

Your rights

You can revoke access in Notion: Settings → My connections. Delete or modify entries directly inside your workspace at any time.
