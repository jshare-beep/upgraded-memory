Privacy Policy

This widget stores timing data in your browser and, when you authorize with Notion, sends time entries to your Notion workspace via the Notion API.

  • No personal data is collected by the site owner beyond what is sent to Notion.
  • OAuth tokens are stored in your browser and used only to call the Notion API via this site's serverless functions.
  • You can revoke access in Notion → Settings → My connections.
